minesweeper

click a mine and lose


How to Play

  • Left click or touch a cell to reveal its contents
  • If the cell contains a mine, you lose
  • If there is no mine, the cell tells you the number of adjacent mines
  • Right-click a cell to flag it as a mine
  • You can toggle 'Flag Mode' to mark multiple cells as mines
  • Reveal all non-mine tiles to win
